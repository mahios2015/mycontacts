**Privacy Policy**

**Introduction**

Welcome to My Contacts. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your personal information and data when you use our app. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this app, you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

**Information We Collect**

**Personal Information**

Contact Information: Users can store their contact information directly in the app instead of using the default contact app. This includes names, phone numbers, email addresses, and other relevant contact details.

Relatives and Important People Information: Users can save information about their relatives and important people separately for quick access and communication.

SMS Information: Users can access and manage their unread SMS messages within the app and read them in their native language.

**Permissions Required**

Contacts Read: To access and manage your contacts within the app. This permission is essential for the core functionality of the app.

SMS Read: To read unread messages within the app and provide the audio feature.

Internet: To provide audio features in your native language and for any updates or necessary downloads.

Download Access: To download necessary language packs or audio files for the app's functionality.

By installing the App, you grant it permission to access your SMS and Call Log as described in this Privacy Policy. You may revoke this permission at any time through your device settings.

**How We Use Your Information**

Store and Manage Contacts: To store your contact information and manage it within the app.

Quick Communication: To enable quick calling and communication with your relatives and important contacts.

Unread Messages: To display unread messages within the app and provide audio playback of these messages in your native language.

Audio Features: To provide audio playback of contact names and SMS messages in several primary Indian languages.

**SMS/Call Log Information**

The SMS and Call Log information accessed by the App is used solely for the purposes mentioned above. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer this information to third parties.

**Data Storage and Security**

Local Storage: All data entered into the My Contacts app is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your contacts or messages to any server.

Data Deletion: If you uninstall the app, all stored data will be removed from your device by default. We do not retain any personal information once the app is uninstalled.

**Sharing of Information**

No Sharing: We do not share your contacts, messages, or any personal information with third parties. All information remains on your device and is accessible only through the My Contacts app.

**User Control and Data Access**

User Control: You have full control over the data you store in My Contacts. You can add, edit, or delete contacts and messages as needed.

Data Access: The app requires specific permissions (contacts read, SMS read, internet, and download access) to function correctly. These permissions are solely used to enhance the app's functionality and user experience.

**Default Policy Content**

No Adult Content: My Contacts does not contain any adult content or materials.

Children's Privacy: My Contacts is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers immediately.

Third-Party Advertisements: My Contacts does not display any third-party advertisements.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify users through the app if significant changes are made. It is recommended to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

 

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the My Contacts app, please contact us at mah4care@gmail.com.

**Conclusion**

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. Thank you for using My Contacts.
